Khana Kaba

Cubic-shaped building located at the center of the Masjid al-Haram mosque in Mecca, Saudi Arabia, which is considered the holiest site in Islam.

Masjid Nabawi

mosque located in Medina, Saudi Arabia, which is the second holiest site in Islam and contains the tomb of Prophet Muhammad..

Abraj Al Bait

megatall skyscraper complex located in Mecca, Saudi Arabia, which includes the world's tallest clock tower.(1,971 feet)

Makkha Masjid

Grand mosque located in Hyderabad, India, which is one of the largest and oldest mosques in the country.

Al-Aqsa

Significant Islamic holy site located in the Old City of Jerusalem, which is considered the third holiest site in Islam.
